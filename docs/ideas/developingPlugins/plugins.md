# Plugins
## The possibility to create plugins for solving specific project's needs.

The idea of making this system plugin-friendly is to make it simpler for specific needs, good use cases could be:

"Kong Plugin, auto manage kong for your group of applications"
or 
"DownDetector test, add your service to our down detector website"

and a lot of other things.

## Plugin stores

For managing specific plugins for specific apps, we could add a store, or more likely a marketplace for the plugins, and a way to add them through it.
